Submission Guidelines

We accept articles on topics that are relevant to the HS, and they must be:

  • Either in Arabic, French or English. Vulgar slang (Tounsi) is not allowed.
  • Politically correct, inoffensive, and intellectually honest.
  • Well sourced in the case of 'informative' articles. Empty claims will not be accepted.
  • Clear, concise and organized with a clear body. Our editorial staff will not be organizing the articles.

Response time: We typically respond within 3-5 days. Expect a delay during exam periods, as the editorial staff is Bac.
